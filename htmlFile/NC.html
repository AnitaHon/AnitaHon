UX/UI Case Study; NaturCycle

Description

NaturCycle wants to establish a distinctive brand presence reflecting their commitment to sustainability while ensuring user engagement and active participation. This case study was conducted to develop an interactive and engaging visual journey to walk users through NaturCycle’s approach.
